Portfolio Page

This page would display the art that I've worked on. Specifically, the games and assets that I have created. This is still a work in progress, but here are some of the games I've made in the meantime.

This is a game that questions the player: what does it mean to keep hope alive? FIRE KEEPER is an adventure game filled with puzzles, relentless enemies and dire resource management.

Brent Porter has been investigating the disappearance of his daugther for 6 months, has he finally tracked her down? 19 Blaine Avenue is a PS1-inspired Thriller game where players must solve puzzles, connect the pieces of a dense narrative and try to solve the case of Porter's missing daughter.

A digital version of an abstract strategy game, 90s Twist is a funky take on X's and O's where players must sabotage each other to win!

A cozy item management game where players can buy, store, sell and rent items. Try your best to earn $300!
