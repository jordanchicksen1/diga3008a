Example:


Nintendo Store Sandton


Pros:


All of the pages are easy to access, very navigable. The colour palette that is easy on the eyes, yet still highlights important features. The buttons and links are easily clickable and viewable due to their size.

Cons:


It's a little busy sometimes. The amount of things that users have access to can be overwhelming. So elements seem redundant, it almost feels like there are too many ways to access the same page. However it is convenient. Sometimes, animations can be a little distracting.
