WEEK 3

Outlining key terms:

Before beginning this discussion, it would be worthwhile to outline the definitions of some of the key terms that will be used in this discussion.


Alignment

To articulate a goal in a succinct and simple way, and to make it achievable.


User:

A person who visits the site, who the site is made for. The target audience of the site.


Therefore, user alignment would who would be visiting my site and what would they be doing.

My users would hopefully be potential collaborators or employers. What those users would be able to do is view the art that I create, and be able to contact me regarding that art if they so please.

Mapping out my sites content:

Blogs

These would be more informal thoughts, that are still carefully considered, but not pieces of writing that are mulled over endlessly. Shorter-form writing where I give my quick thoughts on any given topic.

Essays

Long-form writing that deals with my thoughts on interactive media, filled with carefully considered opinions and research on any given topic related to interactive media.

Design

This will display my work-in-progress artworks and/or UI/UX visual updates for this website.

Profile

This will display my contact information, education, attributes and work experience.

Portfolio

This will display my released works, specifically my Itch.io page, and the things that I have put on there.

Each of these sections will have their own folder, hub page and content pages.
