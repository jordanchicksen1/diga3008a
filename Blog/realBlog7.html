Week 7 Blog Post

Example:

Nintendo Store Sandton

Pros:

  • Easily navigable to all of the different pages
  • Colour palette that is easy on the eyes, yet still highlights important features.
  • Buttons and links are easily clickable and viewable.

Cons:

  • It's a little busy
  • So elements seem redundant, it almost feels like there are too many ways to access the same page. However it is convenient.
  • Sometimes, animations can be a little distracting.
